My Love For Hockey

In 6th Grade, a family from Boston moved into my neighborhood, and one thing was obvious about them: they LOVED hockey. It felt like everytime I went outside, one of their kids was skating a round with a hockey stick and puck. One day I decided to join them and my love for hockey began. Long story short, I decided to try to learn how to play hockey at a local rink and got hooked. Ever since them I have loved playing and watching the sport of Hockey!
